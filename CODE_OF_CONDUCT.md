# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the security community

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- Harassment, trolling, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Using this tool's knowledge for offensive exploitation of systems without authorization
- Other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take corrective action, including temporary or permanent bans, in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior via GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
